Steve Jobs

A visionary

Steve Jobs, Co-founder of Apple, who changed the view of internet after introducing I phone

Timeline of Steve Jobs' life

  • 1955:Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco to Joanne Carole Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali.
  • 1961:The Jobs family moves to Mountain View, Calif., part of what would later become known as Silicon Valley.
  • 1968:Got the internship at Hewlett-Packard.
  • 1970:Meets future Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
  • 1972:Graduates from Homestead High School in Cupertino, Calif., and enrolls at Reed College in Portland, Ore., only to drop out a semester later
  • 1974:Begins a brief stint as an engineer at Atari.
  • 1976:Co-founds Apple Computer with Wozniak and Ronald Wayne.
  • 1977:Apple incorporates.
  • 1977:Releases the Apple II, the first commercially available personal computer.
  • 1980:Apple goes public.
  • 1985: Jobs resigns as Apple chairman.
  • 1986:Buys the Graphics Group division of Lucasfilm that becomes Pixar Animation Studios.
  • 1988:NeXT Computer releases its first computer.
  • 1993:NeXT discontinues hardware business, gets into software instead.
  • 1995:Becomes Pixar's president and CEO. Later in the year, Jobs brings Pixar public, one week after the release of "Toy Story".
  • 1996:Returns to Apple.
  • 1997:Becomes the CEO.
  • 1997:Introduces Apple Store.
  • 1998:Introduces I MAC.
  • 2001:Introduces I Tunes, MAC OS X & I Pod.
  • 2005:Introduces I Pod shuffle and NANO.
  • 2006:Disney buys Pixar. Steave Jobs joins Disney's board as top share holder.
  • 2007:Introduced I Phone and I pod touch.
  • 2008:Introduced Macbook Air.
  • 2010:Introduces I pad.
  • 2011:Gives a keynote speech at his last public appearance and resigned as CEO before his passing in September.

Steve showed me—and all of us—what it means to serve humanity. We miss him, today and every day, and we’ll never forget the example he set for us.,
